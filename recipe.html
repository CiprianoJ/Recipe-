Baked Alaska

Ingredients

  • 6 white eggs
  • 2 quart bricks of ice cream
  • 6 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • Thin sheet of sponge cake

Steps

  1. Make meringue of eggs and sugar
  2. Cover a cutting board with white paper
  3. Lay sponge cake on top
  4. Turn ice cream on cake (it should extend one-half inch beyond cream)
  5. Cover with meringue, and spread smoothly
  6. Place on oven grate and brown quickly in hot oven
  7. Slip from paper on ice cream platter
